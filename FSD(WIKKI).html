SCOTT FORSTALL

Scott Forstall is an American software engineer best known for leading the development of iOS at Apple Inc. He joined Apple in the late 1990s and played a key role in creating the user interface for Mac OS X and later the iPhone's operating system. Forstall was a close associate of Steve Jobs and was instrumental in the development of iOS's skeuomorphic design, which mimicked real-world textures and objects. He left Apple in 2012 after a major management restructuring. Since then, he has kept a relatively low profile, working as an advisor and producer on Broadway.
